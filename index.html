Growing Knowledge — Cultivating the Future

Open Green Lab Algarve explores how we can live in balance with nature. We plant, learn, and share — from forest gardens and local ecology to solar and water systems that respect the land around us.

About

Open Green Lab Algarve is an independent community initiative based in the mountains of Monchique. We blend ecological design, permaculture, and gentle technology to nurture resilience and creativity. Our aim is to reconnect people with land and each other.
